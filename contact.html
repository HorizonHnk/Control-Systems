Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the platform and control systems

Is everything really free?

Yes! All video tutorials, resources, and the AI assistant are completely free. My mission is to make quality engineering education accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

How accurate is the AI assistant?

The AI assistant uses Google's Gemini 2.0-flash model and is trained on control systems concepts. While highly accurate, I recommend double-checking critical calculations and using it as a learning aid rather than a replacement for understanding the concepts.

Can I use these resources for my university studies?

Absolutely! The content is designed to complement university control systems courses. Many students use these resources to better understand their coursework and prepare for exams.

How often is new content added?

I try to add new videos and resources regularly, typically 1-2 new videos per month. The AI assistant and interactive tools are continuously updated with new features and improvements.

Do you offer 1-on-1 tutoring?

Currently, I focus on creating content that helps as many students as possible. However, I'm always open to discussing collaboration opportunities and ways to help students learn more effectively.

How can I support the project?

The best way to support Control Systems Mastery is by sharing it with other students, providing feedback, and participating in the community. Your success stories and testimonials help inspire others to learn!
